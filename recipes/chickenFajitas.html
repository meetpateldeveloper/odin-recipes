Mint Chicken

Description

Chicken fajitas are a flavorful Mexican dish made with grilled or sautéed chicken strips, peppers, and onions, served with warm tortillas and optional toppings.

Ingrediants

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Flour or corn tortillas

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced chicken to the skillet and season with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook for about 5-7 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
  3. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.
  4. Add another tbsp of olive oil to the skillet and add the sliced peppers and onions. Season with salt and pepper and sauté for about 5 minutes, until they are tender and slightly charred.
  5. Add the cooked chicken back to the skillet with the peppers and onions, and stir to combine.
  6. Serve the chicken and peppers mixture with warm flour or corn tortillas.
  7. Top with optional toppings such as sour cream, shredded cheese, guacamole, salsa, and lime wedges, as desired.
